Welcome to Your First UX Task

You're working at JobHunter.ai, a job search platform. Users are dropping off at the "About Me" field in the profile creation flow. Your mission: figure out why and propose a quick fix.

What you'll do:

  • Read the problem brief and analytics
  • Choose the best hypothesis about the drop-off
  • Pick a practical UX solution

Takes about 15 minutes • No signup required

1

The Problem

JH

JobHunter.ai

Job search platform

The Issue: 38% of users who start creating a profile drop off at the "About Me" field. This is the highest drop-off point in the entire onboarding flow. If users don't complete their profile, they can't get personalized job recommendations.

Analytics Data:

  • • 62% of users reach the "About Me" field
  • • Only 38% complete it
  • • Average time spent on field: 2.3 minutes
  • • Most users click "Skip" or leave the page

Your task: Think about why users might be dropping off. What could be causing this?
