🚀 What you'll do inside

You'll step into the role of a Copywriter at JobHunter.ai, a fictional startup from the ProfPilot universe.

Over 3 interactive days you will:

Read a real-style creative brief.

Chat with your mentor Alex and a cross-functional team.

Make choices, write lines, and get instant feedback.

Present your final concept — just like in a real agency sprint.

Everything happens in dialogue

— no slides, no tasks to "study".

You're not learning how to write copy — you're living the job for 3 days.

💡 Who it's for

For those who want to try creative work, not read about it.

If you've ever looked at ads and thought, "I could write better," or you're curious how ideas become campaigns — this simulation is for you.

Career changers

exploring creative jobs.

Marketers, founders, or designers

who want to understand the logic behind good copy.

Anyone who's tired of theory

and wants to feel how creative work happens day-to-day.

💬 Why it's awesome

Because it's not a course.

It's a career test-drive: you experience the rhythm, challenges, and energy of the role — and decide if it's really for you.

You'll see what parts of the work excite you

what feels natural, and what doesn't.

You finish with:

• A real sense of what a copywriter actually does.

• A mini-portfolio piece you can show.

• And maybe the answer to a big question:

👉 "Is this the kind of work where I feel alive?"
