FAQ

Answers to common questions about ProfPilot

How long does it take to complete a simulator? +
Each simulator is designed for short, daily sessions — usually 20–40 minutes per day. It depends on how much time you spend reflecting and solving tasks.
Do I need prior experience? +
No prior experience is required. The simulators are built to introduce you to the essentials of a role in a practical, accessible way.
What do I get at the end? +
A structured experience of 3 real-life days in the role, tasks completed, guided feedback, and a clearer idea of whether this career path is right for you.
Is this like a course or bootcamp? +
Not exactly. Bootcamps often last months, cost thousands, and may not deliver realistic outcomes. ProfPilot is short, affordable, and focused on testing whether the career fits you before you commit.
How much does it cost? +
Each simulator costs $15. It’s less than a dinner out, and gives you a clear sense of whether a job is worth pursuing further.
Can I access simulators anytime? +
Yes. Once purchased, you can start whenever you want and go at your own pace.
