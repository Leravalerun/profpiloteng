Why ProfPilot

People spend thousands of dollars and months of effort on career change programs. Bootcamps often promise high salaries, but research shows only about 27% of graduates land a job in the new field within 6 months (Course Report, 2023).

At the same time, student loan debt for reskilling is growing worldwide. Many quit their jobs, take loans, and still fail to transition.

With ProfPilot you can test‑drive a profession before risking your time and money. Do real tasks, get guided feedback, and decide if the role fits you.
