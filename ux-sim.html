Day 1 — Problem Discovery & Daily Routines

Your first day on the design team

Welcome to your first day as a UX Designer! Today you'll experience the real rhythm of design work: morning routines, team meetings, coffee breaks, and deep analysis sessions. Your AI mentor will be with you throughout the day to guide and support you.

Morning Activities (9:00 AM)

1

Office arrival & setup

Set up your laptop, pour coffee, check emails

2

Team sync meeting

Discuss registration funnel analytics with data analyst

3

Initial data review

Skim meeting notes, start ambient playlist for focus

Coffee Break (10:30 AM)

Team gathering: The team meets in the lounge for coffee and casual conversation. New interns join, and everyone chats about last night's TV show.

Quick Brainstorming:

How can we make onboarding less intimidating for new users? Create two fun microcopy ideas for onboarding tips.

Late Morning Work (11:00 AM)

1

Design tasks

Deliver SVG icons, help fix Figma bugs

2

Deep data analysis

Dive into heatmaps and click maps tracking user actions

3

Team collaboration

Join Figma Jam session, start Slack polls

Lunch Break (1:00 PM)

Team lunch: UI designers, developers, and HR gather around the table. Someone shares a funny meme about dark mode debates.

Lunch Discussion:

Discuss new Figma plugin features and get inspired for micro-tests. What would happen if we make the 'Save' button bright red?

Afternoon Sprint (2:00 PM)

1

Mini sprint kick-off

Team debates quick UX tweaks and task assignments

2

Wireframe creation

Create two variations for the 'About Me' section

3

Content writing

Write beginner's guide: 'How to Not Be Afraid to Write About Yourself'

Second Coffee Break (4:00 PM)

Afternoon refresh: Second coffee break with pastries. An informal debate starts: should the 'About Me' field be optional at all?

Team Discussion:

Discuss ideas for Saturday team hackathon and challenge Ally over help-text length — too detailed or just right?

End of Day (5:00 PM)

1

End-of-day sync

Everyone shares accomplishments and blockers

2

Key insight summary

Summarize the day's main insight in one sentence

3

Documentation

Save all sketches, drafts, and notes for tomorrow
