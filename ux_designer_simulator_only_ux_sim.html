UX Designer Simulator

In this 3‑day simulator, you'll step into the shoes of a UX designer working at a job‑search startup. Your challenge: fix a drop‑off problem in the onboarding questionnaire so more users complete it and get value from the product.

What you'll do

  • Day 1 — Review analytics and click maps to identify where and why users stop.
  • Day 2 — Propose quick, low‑effort UX changes to help them continue.
  • Day 3 — Visualize your solution and pitch it to the team.

Skills you'll practice

  • Analyzing user behavior and funnels
  • Formulating and testing UX hypotheses
  • Writing clear, helpful microcopy
  • Creating quick low‑fidelity concepts

Why try this simulator?

It's a safe, fast way to see if UX design excites you. You'll get realistic tasks, instant feedback, and a feel for how designers solve problems in real products — without needing prior design skills.
